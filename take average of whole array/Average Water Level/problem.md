## Title

Average Water Level

## Slug

average-water-level

## Difficulty

Easy

## Description

Sensors measure the water levels (in cm) of a reservoir daily.  
Find the floor of the average water level.

## Examples

### 1

#### Input

5  
3 10 6 1 8

#### Output
5

#### Explanation

Average = 5.6 → Floor = 5.

### 2

#### Input

4  
7 7 7 7

#### Output
7

## Input Format  

- First line: integer `n` — number of days.  
- Second line: `n` integers — water levels.  

## Output Format  

- A single integer — floor of average water level.  

## Constraints  

- 1 ≤ n ≤ 1000  
- -10⁹ ≤ arr[i] ≤ 10⁹  

## Time Limit

1 second

## Memory Limit

512 MB

## Tags

arrays.
